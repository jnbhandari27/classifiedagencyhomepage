About

We are a team of qualified people having expertise and years of experience in the field of advertising and marketing. We provide wider and fruitful advertising services such as classified advertising along with other promotional activities.

Originally formed in 2018, when three college students from different backgrounds but with similar minds came together with a vision to serve new and emerging businesses of India with their advertising and promotional solutions. Within last two years, we have exponentially grown our business and team from just 3 students to over 50 full-time working professionals who are the best in their respective fields.

We have worked with various large and small brands across the country and have also worked with many international brands. Although our client list is expanding, we aim to provide equal opportunity to each and every client with unmatched customer support.
